Roadal

An app for informing road users on any activities on roads that affect road usage. Different organisation that do projects that affect road usage such as road construction, water and sewage companies are registered and inform users on projects that might affect road usage. Users are also able to report any activity or occurance that affect road usage such as accidents and the like.

On the frot-end HTML, Sass, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Leaflet were used. On the back-end Node.js and Express.js were used. For database management MySQL was used. The app has a custom Content Management System (CMS). However, the published site is only the front-end for the general public.
